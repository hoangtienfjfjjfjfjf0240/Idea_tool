## Idea Comparison

### Pair 1

Gemini 3 Pro: UGC - New Year Bathroom Resolution
- Hook: Woman in clean activewear stands in an old bathroom with a New Year planner, says she wants a new bathroom but has zero design skills.
- Overlay: When you want a new bathroom but have 0 design skills
- Good: Directly uses January/winter and the selected design-skill painpoint.
- Weak: Introduces the app inside the hook early.

GPT-5.4: UGC - New Year bathroom tease
- Hook: Woman in a cream sweater and beanie stands in an outdated bathroom with a planner, asks whether the room should be modern, cozy, or spa, then hides the generated redesign.
- Overlay: I only took 1 photo...
- Good: More natural dialogue, stronger curiosity gap, more realistic UGC.
- Weak: Slower model response.

Winner: GPT-5.4

### Pair 2

Gemini 3 Pro: UGC - Home Depot Fail
- Hook: Husband brings mismatched Home Depot tile samples into the bathroom; wife says they both have zero design skills.
- Overlay: When neither of us knows how to design
- Good: Very realistic US/homeowner conflict, easy to shoot, funny.
- Weak: Painpoint shifts slightly toward bad material matching.

GPT-5.4: Reaction - Pinterest overload bathroom
- Hook: Woman has 147 saved Pinterest bathroom ideas but still cannot decide what fits her real bathroom.
- Overlay: Saved 147 ideas. Still couldn't decide.
- Good: Best fit for the chosen painpoint; highly relatable and realistic.
- Weak: Less dramatic than Home Depot conflict.

Winner: GPT-5.4

### Pair 3

Gemini 3 Pro: UGC - Budget Planning Shock
- Hook: Woman sees a $2,500 designer quote, refuses to pay, then uses the app.
- Overlay: Me vs. a $2500 design quote
- Good: Strong shock value.
- Weak: Most off-config idea; shifts from "không biết thiết kế" to "không muốn trả tiền designer."

GPT-5.4: POV - Husband asks which style
- Hook: Husband asks which bathroom style she wants; she says that is exactly the problem because everything looks good separately and weird together.
- Overlay: When you know you want a new bathroom... but not the style
- Good: Very close to the selected painpoint and easy to film as UGC/POV.
- Weak: Needs a clearer first-frame visual to be instantly scroll-stopping.

Winner: GPT-5.4

## Verdict

GPT-5.4 wins quality for this exact configuration.

Use Gemini 3 Pro when speed matters or when you need more dramatic concept variation.
Use GPT-5.4 when correctness to selected filters, realistic UGC, and painpoint discipline matter more.
